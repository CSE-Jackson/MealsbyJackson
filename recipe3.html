Breakfast Burrito

Ingredients:
  • - large flour tortillas.
  • - 6-8 large eggs.
  • - 4-6 slices of bacon (cooked and crumbled) OR 1/2 lb breakfast sausage (browned).
  • - 1-2 cups shredded cheddar, Monterey Jack, or a mix.
  • - 1-2 cups hash browns or diced potatoes.
  • Vegetables (Optional): Diced bell peppers, onions, or spinach.
Instructions:
  1. Step 1: Cook the bacon or sausage in a skillet. If using hash browns, cook them in the same pan until crispy.
  2. Step 2: Whisk eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper. Melt butter in a non-stick skillet over medium-low heat and scramble until just set.
  3. Step 3: Microwave tortillas in a damp paper towel for 30-45 seconds to make them pliable.
  4. Step 4: Layer eggs, meat, potatoes, and a generous amount of cheese in the center of each tortilla.
  5. Step 5: Fold in the sides, roll tightly, and toast in a hot skillet seam-side down for 1-2 minutes until golden brown.
