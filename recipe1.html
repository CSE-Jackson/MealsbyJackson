Burrito Bowl

Ingredients:

- 1 pound of ground beef

- 1-2 cups of rice

- 1 packet of taco seasoning

- Any toppings you desire, I reccomend shredded lettuce and cheese.

Instructions:
  1. Step 1: Melt butter on the pan or apply cooking oil.
  2. Step 2: Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Step 3: While cooking the ground beef, add the taco seasoning and any other desired spices.
  4. Step 4: Cook the rice however you prefer (stovetop, microwave, etc.).
  5. Step 5: Once the beef is fully cooked, add the rice to the pan and mix together.
  6. Step 6: Add any desired toppings and enjoy!
