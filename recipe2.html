Add simple toppings to this meal as the orange sauce makes up for the most of the flavor. Sprinkling chives or seaseme seeds can add a nice touch to the meal.

Orange Chicken

Ingredients: For the Orange Sauce:

- Orange Juice

- Soy Sauce

- Vinegar and spices

- Thickening agent (cornstarch)

- Vegetable oil and fresh garlic and ginger. (Spices of your choice)

Other Ingredients:

- Chicken (cut into bite-sized pieces)

- Salt and pepper

- Rice (I prefer jasmine rice)

Instructions:
  1. Step 1: Add the orange sauce ingredient to a cup and stir well
  2. Step 2: Heat up a skillet on medium-low heat and simmer the sauce until it thickens
  3. Step 3: Prepare the chicken in a bowl and toss the chicken with flour, and your preferred spices if desired
  4. Step 4: Add oil to a pan and cook the chicken until golden brown and fully cooked through
  5. Step 5: Once the chicken is fully cooked, add the sauce to the pan and mix together
  6. Step 6: Prepare your rice and serve the chicken over it!
